Mental health is an essential component of overall well-being and plays a crucial role in our ability to lead a fulfilling life.

It encompasses our emotional, psychological, and social well-being and affects how we think, feel, and act. Good mental health allows us to form positive relationships, use our abilities to reach our potential, and deal with life’s challenges.

Mental health issues can arise from a variety of factors such as genetics, life experiences, or brain chemistry. It is important to prioritize mental health and seek help when needed because untreated mental health issues can lead to negative consequences such as decreased quality of life, strained relationships, or physical health problems. There are many ways to improve mental health such as therapy, medication, exercise, or mindfulness practices. It is important to find what works for each individual and make mental health a priority. By taking care of our mental health, we can lead happier and more fulfilling lives.

Our History

Our Story Having found the similar interest and understanding of the practices in psychotherapy, we decided on creating a space together which resonated with our ideas of practice. Thus, taking a ‘pause’ from our individual work helped us create “Alpaviraam”.Alpaviraam (Comma&-punctuation in English) literally means a pause between parts of a sentence, or a small interval. There is always an Alpaviraam, when the sentences clutter and stop making sense as well as when there is something more to look forward to. When used properly, these commas help making meaning out of the sentences much like how we intend to use this space for mental health.
